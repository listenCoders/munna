ChatGPT: A Conversational AI Milestone

When OpenAI released ChatGPT in November 2022, it felt as though the entire landscape of the internet and possibly even modern technology had yet again made a significant leap forward. ChatGPT has become popular with millions of diverse users, offering creativity and content generation as well as data analysis and custom-built GPTs1.

However, ChatGPT isn't without its challenges. Some users have encountered problematic answers or biased behavior. Teachers, in particular.

As ChatGPT continues to evolve, it remains a fascinating and powerful tool, sparking conversations and pushing the boundaries of what AI can achieve.
